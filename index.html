Frequently Asked Questions




What is sea pollution?

Sea pollution refers to harmful substances like plastic, chemicals, and waste entering our oceans, causing damage to marine life and ecosystems.

What’s in it for me?

By helping protect our oceans, you’re contributing to a healthier planet, preserving biodiversity, and securing cleaner waters for future generations.

How does Toun’Sea use donations?

We use donations for clean-up events, awareness campaigns, and establishing marine conservation zones.

Can I volunteer?

Yes! We have many volunteer opportunities, from beach cleanups to educational programs.

How can I help?

You can participate in our fundraisers, join a beach cleanup, donate, or raise awareness about marine conservation.
